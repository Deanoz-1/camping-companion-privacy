Camping Companion — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Camping Companion ("the app") is an iOS app for UK campers, caravanners and motorhome owners, operated by Dean Sellars ("we", "us"). Contact: deanoz@me.com.

What we collect and why

Account details. Your email address and password, used solely to sign you in. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider and are never visible to us.

Rig and maintenance details. Information you enter about your vehicle — name, type, dimensions, tyres, service records, fuel fill-ups, subscriptions and documents. Stored so the app can show it back to you.

Location. Used, with your permission, to: attach a location to hazard reports you submit; centre the hazard map near you; find nearby campsites and LPG stations; check you in at a site; fetch local weather; and record a last-known location if you raise a stolen-rig alert. The app never tracks your location in the background.

Photos. Photos you choose to attach to posts, journal entries or your rig library are uploaded to our storage. Journal and rig photos are private to your account; photos on community posts are visible to other users.

Content you share. Posts, comments, likes, hazard reports, hazard votes, campsite check-ins and stolen-rig alerts are visible to other users of the app (displayed with your rig name, not your email).

Where it lives

Data is stored with Supabase (our database, authentication and file-storage provider). Route lookups send your typed origin and destination to Google (Places and Directions APIs) to find and route between locations. Weather uses Open-Meteo and campsite/LPG lookups use OpenStreetMap/Overpass, both receiving approximate coordinates only — never your identity. We do not sell your data, and there is no advertising or analytics tracking in the app.

Retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account exists. You can delete individual posts, comments, photos, journal entries and records at any time in the app. Deleting your account (My Rig → Delete Account) permanently and immediately erases your account, your content, your photos and your personal records from our systems. Community hazard reports you submitted are removed along with the account.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you may request access to, correction of, or erasure of your personal data at any time — use the in-app tools or email deanoz@me.com.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

If this policy changes materially we will update this page and note it in the app's release notes.
